TEAM  REPORTS

 

 

Fé  16 Hurling :   We defeated Russell Rvs. By 5-9 to 5-8 in a thriller before Watergrasshill beat us 2-7 to 0-6 in the semi-final. We had plenty of possession to win the game but didn’t take them. Watergrasshill went on to win the ‘East Cork’.

Fé16 Football  :   Beaten at semi-final stage by Aghada  on scoreline 3-4 to 2-5.

 

Fé 15  Hurling  :     Very unlucky to be beaten in the opening round by St. Catherines 1-6 to 0-8.

Fé 15  Football  :     Won the championship when defeating Glenville after an absorbing final back in March on the scoreline of 1-5 to 1-3.

 

Fé 14  Hurling  :   A very busy year with Féile in Cork. We hosted Navan O’Mahony’s from Meath. We played three games and got one victory. We also participated in the parade of teams in Cork and overall it was a tremendous success. We were defeated by Fr. O Neills in the semi-final of championship by 5-5 to 4-7. It looked as if we would cause a major shock when leading by eight points with ten minutes to go but late goals denied us victory. We await the final of the Paddy O’Doherty tournament where Watergrasshill will be our opponents. This will take place after Christmas.

Fé 14 Football  :  Well beaten after a disappointing performance against Killeagh/Itas who went on to win the championship.

 

Fé 13  Hurling  :   East Cork Champions after victories over Castlelyons, Russell Rvs. and overcoming Cobh in a thrilling final. The score in the final was 6-4 to 4-8.

Fé  13  Football  :  Reached the final only to go under to favourites Castlelyons 5-7 to 4-5 after a great display.

Fé  12  Hurling  :  Defeated by Castlelyons in the semi-final by 2-6 to 1-8 again following a pulsating contest.

Fé  12  Football  :  Once again Castlelyons got the better of us in another memorable encounter in the East Cork Final. A late goal gave them the title by four points 1-7 to 1-3.

 

Fé  11  Hurling  :  Run in a league format we beat Cobh, Castlelyons and Youghal but were defeated by Killeagh and Fr O’Neills and therefore didn’t qualify for final. Won the Plate final of the Mossie Twomey tournament when gaining a terrific over St Finbarrs 2-2 to 1-2.

Fé 11  Football :  Went through the campaign unbeaten and won the final over Fr O’Neills by 0-9 to 1-1.

 

 

CLUB HURLING  SKILLS  CONTESTS

 

 

Paddy Walsh Memorial for players on their final year at Fé 14 was won by Conor Hayes.

 

Michael Griffin Memorial for players on their final year at Fé12 was won by Martin Hennessy.

 

Next year 2006 we plan to hold a similar competition within the club for players on their final year at Fé 16.

 

 

Our club long puck competiton was held on the May bank holiday and was an outstanding success once again.

 

 

 

CLUB  TRIPS

 

Again we headed for Croke Park with 110 travelling to the All-Ireland hurling semi-final when Cork defeated Clare. Our victorious fe 13 panel also went to Tralee on a day trip. Both trips went very well.

 

 

CLUB  SOCIAL

 

In conjunction with Dungourney Camogie Club we had in 220 in attendance at our recent social which turned out to be a fantastic night. Guests on the night were Donal Og Cusack and Mary O’Connor.
